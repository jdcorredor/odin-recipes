Cinnamon Rolls

Description

These cinnamon rolls are the perfect treat for a cozy weekend breakfast or brunch. Soft, fluffy dough swirled with a generous amount of cinnamon and sugar, then topped with a luscious glaze. They’re sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • For the glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons milk, ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add in the melted butter, egg, remaining sugar, and salt. Gradually mix in the flour until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Spread melted butter over the surface, then sprinkle with a mixture of cinnamon and brown sugar.
  5. Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut into 12 equal pieces. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish.
  6. Cover and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  7. Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  8. While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by mixing powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  9. Once the rolls are out of the oven, drizzle the glaze over the warm rolls. Serve and enjoy!
